## Welcome & Overview

### What is SolarVoice AI?

SolarVoice AI is your intelligent voice assistant for solar construction sites. Think of it as having an experienced supervisor who:
- Speaks your language (English, Spanish, Portuguese, Mandarin, and more)
- Never forgets important safety protocols
- Instantly connects you with the right people and resources
- Works 24/7 to keep your projects running smoothly

### Who Uses SolarVoice AI?

- **Crew Chiefs** - Manage teams and assignments
- **Field Workers** - Report progress and issues
- **Safety Officers** - Monitor and respond to incidents
- **Project Managers** - Track overall progress
- **Equipment Operators** - Coordinate resources
- **Quality Inspectors** - Document inspections

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Download the App

#### For Mobile Devices
1. **iPhone**: Search "SolarVoice AI" in the App Store
2. **Android**: Search "SolarVoice AI" in Google Play Store
3. Tap "Install" and wait for download

#### For Tablets/Computers
- Visit [app.solarvoice.ai](https://app.solarvoice.ai) in your web browser
- Bookmark for easy access

### Step 2: Create Your Account

1. **Open the app**
2. **Tap "Get Started"**
3. **Enter your information:**
   - Phone number
   - Full name
   - Company name
   - Job role
   - Preferred language

4. **Verify your phone** 
   - You'll receive a 6-digit code
   - Enter it when prompted

5. **Set up voice recognition**
   - Follow the prompts to say a few phrases
   - This helps SolarVoice recognize your voice better

### Step 3: Join Your Team

1. **Enter your team code** (provided by your supervisor)
2. **Select your primary job site**
3. **Choose your certifications** (forklift, electrical, etc.)
4. **Set your availability status**

## Your First Day

### Morning Check-In

When you arrive at the job site:

1. **Say: "Good morning SolarVoice"**
   - The system will check you in
   - You'll hear today's safety briefing
   - Get your assignments for the day

2. **Confirm your assignment**
   - Say "Accept" if everything looks good
   - Say "Question" if you need clarification

### Your First Voice Command

Try these simple commands to get comfortable:

1. **"What's my assignment today?"**
   - Hear your tasks and location

2. **"Where should I report?"**
   - Get directions to your work area

3. **"Who's on my team?"**
   - Learn who you're working with

### Throughout the Day

Use voice commands for:
- **Progress updates**: "Completed panel installation in row 5"
- **Getting help**: "Need assistance with heavy lifting"
- **Safety concerns**: "Wet conditions in Zone A"
- **Break times**: "Taking lunch break"

### End of Day

Before leaving:
1. **Say: "End of day report"**
2. **Confirm hours worked**
3. **Report any issues or concerns**
4. **Say: "Checking out"**

## Tips for Success

### Voice Command Best Practices

1. **Speak Naturally**
   - Talk like you would to a coworker
   - No need for robot-speak

2. **Be Specific**
   - Include location: "Need ladder at Zone C"
   - Include quantity: "Delivered 100 panels"
   - Include timing: "Break for 30 minutes"

3. **Use in Noisy Environments**
   - Cup hand around microphone
   - Face away from loud equipment
   - Use noise-canceling mode

### Common Scenarios

#### Scenario 1: Reporting an Issue
```
You: "Problem with inverter 3"
SolarVoice: "What kind of problem are you seeing?"
You: "Error code E24 on display"
SolarVoice: "Technical support notified. Someone will arrive in 15 minutes."
```

#### Scenario 2: Coordinating with Team
```
You: "Where is the electrical crew?"
SolarVoice: "Electrical crew is at Zone D, finishing connections."
You: "Tell them I need help in Zone C when they're done"
SolarVoice: "Message sent to electrical crew lead."
```

#### Scenario 3: Safety Concern
```
You: "Unsafe condition - loose scaffolding"
SolarVoice: "Location of the unsafe scaffolding?"
You: "Northwest corner of Zone B"
SolarVoice: "Safety alert issued. Area marked as restricted until fixed."
```

### Time-Saving Features

1. **Quick Commands**
   - "Status" - Get full update
   - "Help" - Immediate assistance
   - "Repeat" - Hear last message again

2. **Shortcuts**
   - Double-tap earpiece for emergency
   - Long press for language switch
   - Swipe for command history

3. **Offline Mode**
   - Basic commands work without internet
   - Syncs when connection returns
   - Emergency features always available

### Troubleshooting

#### Can't Log In?
1. Check phone number is correct
2. Verify team code with supervisor
3. Ensure internet connection
4. Contact support: 1-800-SOLAR-AI

#### Voice Commands Not Working?
1. Check microphone permissions
2. Reduce background noise
3. Speak slower and clearer
4. Try alternative phrasing

#### App Crashes?
1. Update to latest version
2. Restart your device
3. Clear app cache
4. Reinstall if needed
