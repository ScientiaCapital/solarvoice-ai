# SolarVoice AI - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is SolarVoice AI?
SolarVoice AI is an intelligent voice assistant designed specifically for solar construction sites. It helps crews communicate, stay safe, and work more efficiently using natural voice commands in multiple languages.

### Who can use SolarVoice AI?
Anyone working on a solar construction site can use SolarVoice AI, including:
- Field crews and installers
- Crew chiefs and supervisors
- Safety officers
- Project managers
- Equipment operators
- Quality inspectors
- Subcontractors

### What languages does SolarVoice AI support?
Currently supported languages include:
- English
- Spanish (Español)
- Portuguese (Português)
- Mandarin Chinese (中文)
- French (Français)
- German (Deutsch)

You can switch languages at any time by saying "Change language to [language name]" in any supported language.

### Do I need special equipment?
No special equipment required! SolarVoice AI works with:
- Any smartphone (iPhone or Android)
- Basic feature phones (limited features)
- Tablets
- Smart safety helmets (optional)
- Bluetooth headsets (optional)

### I don't have a team code. What should I do?
Contact your supervisor or project manager for your team code. If your company isn't using SolarVoice AI yet, have them contact us at sales@solarvoice.ai or 1-800-SOLAR-AI.

Yes! Your account works on multiple devices. Just log in with your phone number on any device. Your settings and preferences will sync automatically.

During initial setup, you'll be asked to read a few phrases. For better recognition:
- Speak in your normal voice

## Voice Commands

### Do I need to memorize specific commands?
No! SolarVoice AI understands natural language. Just speak normally as you would to a coworker. For example:
- "I need help" 
- "Where's the electrical team?"
- "It's too hot to work safely"

All of these work without memorizing exact phrases.

### What if SolarVoice doesn't understand me?
Try these tips:
1. Speak a bit slower and clearer
2. Include more details (location, equipment type, etc.)
3. Reduce background noise if possible
4. Check that your microphone isn't covered
5. Say "Help" for command examples

### Can I use voice commands in noisy environments?
Yes! SolarVoice AI is optimized for construction sites:
- Advanced noise cancellation technology
- Works with noise levels up to 105 dB
- Cup your hand around the microphone for better results
- Use a Bluetooth headset for best performance

### How do I know if SolarVoice heard my command?
You'll get immediate feedback:
- Audio confirmation tone
- Visual indicator on screen
- Voice response confirming your command
- Vibration feedback (if enabled)

## Safety Features

### What happens when I say "Emergency"?
Saying "Emergency" triggers immediate action:
1. Your location is instantly shared with supervisors
2. Emergency services are notified if needed
3. All nearby crew members are alerted
4. The safety officer is immediately contacted
5. An incident report is automatically started

### Can SolarVoice AI prevent accidents?
While SolarVoice AI can't physically prevent accidents, it helps by:
- Providing real-time safety alerts
- Reminding crews about PPE requirements
- Warning about weather hazards
- Facilitating quick emergency response
- Tracking safety compliance

### How does the weather alert system work?
SolarVoice AI monitors weather continuously:
- Lightning alerts: Automatic evacuation notices
- High wind warnings: Equipment securing reminders
- Heat advisories: Hydration and break reminders
- Rain/snow alerts: Surface safety warnings

### Is my location always tracked?
Location tracking is used only for:
- Emergency response
- Work zone assignments
- Safety compliance
- You can view privacy settings in the app

## Troubleshooting

### The app won't open. What should I do?
1. Check internet connection
2. Restart your device
3. Update to latest app version
4. Clear app cache (Settings > Apps > SolarVoice > Clear Cache)
5. Reinstall if necessary

### Voice commands aren't working properly.
Try these steps:
1. Check microphone permissions (Settings > Apps > SolarVoice > Permissions)
2. Complete voice training again
3. Speak slower and more clearly
4. Update the app
5. Contact support if issues persist

### I forgot my login information.
No problem:
1. Tap "Forgot Login" on sign-in screen
2. Enter your phone number
3. Receive verification code
4. Create new password

### The app is running slowly.
To improve performance:
1. Close other apps
2. Clear app cache
3. Free up phone storage
4. Turn off video features if not needed
5. Use low power mode

### I'm getting wrong information or responses.
Please help us improve:
1. Say "Report error" immediately
2. Describe what went wrong
3. Our team will investigate
4. You'll receive follow-up within 24 hours
